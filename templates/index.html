About this Project

Data on environmental indicators is often just displayed as it is. We see the numbers as they are. Sometimes in graphs or in comparison.

This project aims to uncover insights behind data on SDG 7 using machine learning techniques.

What is SDG7?

SDG7 is the seventh of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. The goals are part of the Agenda 2030 and a number of them is thus supposed to be achieved by 2030. The 7th goal, which is the focus of this project, is the goal:

Affordable and clean energy

All goals are further broken down into smaller targets, which in turn include indicators. The indicators are intended to help measure the progress on the goals.

The SDG7 Targets and Indicators

More precisely: By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable and modern energy services

Indicators:
  • Proportion of population with access to electricity
  • Proportion of population with primary reliance on clean fuels and technology

More precisely: By 2030, increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix

Indicators:
  • Renewable energy share in the total final energy consumption

More precisely: By 2030, double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency

Indicators:
  • Energy intensity measured in terms of primary energy and GDP

More precisely: By 2030, enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology, including renewable energy, energy efficiency and advanced and cleaner fossil-fuel technology, and promote investment in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology

Indicators:
  • International financial flows to developing countries in support of clean energy research and development and renewable energy production, including in hybrid systems

More precisely: By 2030, expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern and sustainable energy services for all in developing countries, in particular least developed countries, small island developing States, and land-locked developing countries, in accordance with their respective programmes of support

Indicators:
  • Installed renewable energy-generating capacity in developing countries (in watts per capita)

Indicators In This Project

Due to the large amount of missing data in the dataset used for this project, the focus of the project has been narrowed down to the following 4 indicators.



Access to electricity (% of population)
Renewable energy share in the total final energy consumption (%)
Access to clean fuels for cooking
Energy intensity level of primary energy (MJ/$2017 PPP GDP)

Missing Countries

These countries have been removed from the analysis due to a large number of missing values across indicators which makes analysis and predictions difficult.

In case of countries for which observations contained some missing values, the missing values were filled using the median of the available data for said country.
